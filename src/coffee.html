Civet Cat Coffee Beans

The civet cat coffee, or also known as Kopi Luwak or cat poop coffee, is one of the most expensive coffee in the world. The palm civet usually prefer to eat the ripest coffee cherries and during digestion a unique fermentation occurs which give it its special flavour. It certainly is a must try cup of coffee.

ROAST TYPE

Roast Level

Dark Roast

Coffee will generally have a bitter and smoky or even burnt taste. Most dark roast are used for expresso blends.

Medium Roast

More caffine as compared to dark roast equipped with more balanced flavour, aroma and acidity.

Light Roast

Contains higher levels of caffine and retains more original flavours. Has a toasted grain taste and pronounced acidiy.
